Get in Touch

Safety, socialization, and happiness. These are our core principles at Paws & Progress. Contact us today to arrange a site visit and see these principles lived out, bring your furry friend with you and join us in one of our more calm introduction areas for a free hour of play!

Safety, socialization, and happiness are our top priorities. Our dedicated team of trainers is passionate about creating a safe and enjoyable environment for your furry companions to thrive.

What Sets Our Trainers Apart:

  • Safety First: Our trainers undergo rigorous training in canine behavior and safety protocols to ensure the well-being of all dogs under our care. From proper leash handling techniques to emergency procedures, we prioritize safety in every aspect of our operations.
  • Expert Socialization: We understand the importance of socialization for dogs' overall well-being. Our trainers are skilled in facilitating positive interactions between dogs of all breeds and temperaments, creating a harmonious and enriching social environment where your pet can make new friends and build confidence.
  • Tailored Happiness: Every dog is unique, and our trainers take the time to understand each dog's individual needs and preferences. Whether it's engaging in stimulating activities, providing calming cuddles, or simply offering a reassuring presence, our goal is to ensure that every dog leaves our day care with a wagging tail and a smile on their face.

At our dog day care, you can trust that your beloved pet is in capable hands. With a team dedicated to safety, socialization, and happiness, we're committed to providing the ultimate experience for both you and your furry friend.
