DAILY SCHEDULE

At Paws & Progress we offer lots of activities to ensure the well-being and engagement of your dogs throughout the day.

Morning Drop Off: 07:00 - 08:00

  • Welcome and check-in of dogs
  • Quick bathroom break for all dogs
  • Individual playtime for early arrivals

PLAY

09:00 - 11:00

Morning Play Session

Group playtime in the daycare area or outdoor play yard (weather permitting) Activities may include fetch, tug-of-war, or other supervised games Supervisors monitor play to ensure safety and intervene if necessary


15:00 - 17:00

Afternoon Play Session

Another round of supervised group play or individual play sessions Rotating toys and equipment to keep dogs engaged and stimulated.

TRAIN

11:00 - 12:00

Enrichment Activities

Mental stimulation activities such as puzzle toys, scent games, or basic training exercises Individual attention for each dog to reinforce good behavior and provide mental exercise

EAT

08:00 - 09:00

Breakfast Time

Feeding time for dogs Individual or group feeding, depending on dietary needs and compatibility Supervised feeding to ensure safety and proper consumption.


12:00 - 13:00

Lunch Time

Midday feeding for dogs (similar to breakfast routine) Quiet time for dogs to relax and digest their meals


17:00-18:00

Evening Snack and Wind-down

Light snacks or treats provided to dogs before departure Quiet activities such as cuddle time or gentle brushing for dogs winding down

SLEEP

13:00 - 15:00

Afternoon Naptime

Dogs are given a chance to rest in individual kennels or designated quiet areas Soft music or white noise may be played to promote relaxation

Evening Pick Up 18:00 - 19:00

Updates given on all the fun activities had throughout the day.

See you tomorrow for lots more fun!
