MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission is simple: to enrich the lives of dogs and their owners by offering exceptional daycare services that prioritize safety, socialization, and happiness. We understand that leaving your beloved pet in someone else's care can be a daunting task, which is why we go above and beyond to ensure that every dog feels right at home with us.

ABOUT US


At Paws & Progress, we are dedicated to providing a safe, nurturing, and enriching environment where dogs can thrive physically, mentally, and socially. We strive to offer personalized care tailored to each dog's unique needs, ensuring they receive the attention, exercise, and stimulation necessary for a happy and fulfilling experience. Through our commitment to excellence in care, professionalism, and a passion for dogs, we aim to foster strong bonds with our furry clients and their human companions, creating a community built on trust, respect, and joy. Here's what sets us apart:


  • Experienced and caring staff: Our team consists of trained professionals who are dedicated to providing the highest level of care for your dogs. From playtime supervision to administering medications, you can trust that your pet is in good hands with us.

  • Safe and stimulating environment: Our facility is designed with your dog's safety and comfort in mind. We have spacious indoor and outdoor play areas where dogs can run, jump, and play to their heart's content. We also provide plenty of toys, enrichment activities, and socialization opportunities to keep them happy and engaged throughout the day.

  • Personalized attention: We understand that every dog is unique, which is why we tailor our services to meet the individual needs and preferences of each pet. Whether your dog loves group play or prefers solo cuddle time, we'll make sure they receive the personalized care and attention they deserve.

  • Transparent communication: We believe in open and honest communication with our clients. From daily updates and photos to regular check-ins, we'll keep you informed about your dog's activities and well-being while they're in our care.



Thank you for considering Paws & Progress for your dog's daycare needs.

We can't wait to welcome your furry friend into our family!
